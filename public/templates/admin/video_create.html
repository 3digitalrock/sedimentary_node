3DRS Guidelines:

  1. Project(s) must be in the Action, Comedy, or Horror genre(s). These genres can be mixed and matched with other genres, but the main ingredient should contain at least one the three.
  2. No pornographic content.
  3. No home videos of any kind will be excepted. We only except professional content, from low/no budget and up.
  4. Respect the copyright of others. If you have copyrighted material in your project it will not be accepted. However if some does slip through to our site, you, the filmmaker, will be held responsible for it being in the material.
  5. Gratuitous violence will be monitored and subject to being pulled off of the site if deemed to offensive by the community or 3DRS Staff.
Please take these rules seriously. It is to protect you and to create great filmmaking experiences and a viewer community.
